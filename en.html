100% safe

No software. No risk.

q247.live is not an app from a store. It is an ordinary web page that you simply save to your home screen. It has no access to anything on your phone.

Nothing gets installed

It is a static HTML page — no package, no background updates, no app running in your system.

No sign-up

You need no account, e-mail or password. Just open it and use it. We collect nothing about you.

No system access

It can’t see your contacts, location, photos or files. It runs in the browser, separate from the rest of your phone.

Nothing is sent

No data travels to any server. Your choices (theme, language) stay only on your device.

No tracking

No advertising cookies, no trackers. Just the content you came for.

Delete in one move

Not for you? Just delete the icon from your home screen. No uninstalling, no leftovers in the system.
