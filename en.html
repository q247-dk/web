Tip of the day · how & where to meet someone

Cloudy Wednesday? All the more reason to head out and meet people instead of staying in.

Prague’s Wednesday is overcast but a pleasant ~24 °C — weather made for an evening stroll. After work, grab a coffee or a drink in Vinohrady or Vršovice, walk through Stromovka or Riegrovy sady, and simply strike up a conversation. During Pride Month the city feels open — make the most of it.

The best pick of the day for meeting people in real life: from 22:00 Friends Club fires up the Connection party with Naděžda Diason — the colour code tells you who’s single, and meeting someone at the bar is almost a given. And if you’d rather go online, most people in Czechia are on Grindr, Bumble or Tinder — but today it’s really worth heading out. 💛
